willy wonka

who is he?

willy wonka is a fun and mysterious candy factory owner, he makes the most magical treats or sweets like a chocolate river and a never ending bubblegum. willy wonka has short funny workers, he calls them his oompa loompas.

whats his story?

when willy was young, his life was very diffrent, his father was very a strict dentist who didnt let willy eat candy, because he thought it was bad for his teeth, but one day wonka secretly tried some candy without his father knowing and he loved it! he became so obsessed with candy that he decided to make a candy factory but he had to sadly leave his family. although he missed his family, he followed his heart and thats what made him so special.

what did he do with his factory?

when willy made his factory, people wanted to steal his mysterious chocolate bars and candies, so willy shut the factory down, however a few years later he opened it again and decided he would let 5 special people into his factory, but for you to be let in the factory by wonka, you needed to find a golden ticket in one of his chocolates. 5 lucky kids found these ticktes and each child got to explore his factory with willy wonka, but there was a catch. there were rules in the factory and 4 of the kids didnt follow them and they got into trouble. one of them even turned into a huge blueberry even though willy wonka warned her. in the end one kid that followed the rules, charlie, won a big prize, willy wonka gifted him his whole factory! he made this decision because he realized he was getting old and he would soon pass away. willy made sure to give his factory to someone that would take good care of his factory, and charlie was the chosen one. this shows how kind and caring wonka is, and that is exactly why i decided to make my project about him.
